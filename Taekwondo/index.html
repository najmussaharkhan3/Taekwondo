Your Journey in Taekwondo Begins Here

Our experienced instructors emphasize not just physical training but also the development of character, discipline, and respect. We believe that through taekwondo, our students can achieve personal growth and forge lasting friendships. Join us on this exciting journey toward strength, confidence, and empowerment!

Traditional Taekwondo

Focuses on the original techniques, forms (hyung), and philosophy of taekwondo as taught by its founders. Emphasizes discipline, respect, and martial arts etiquette.

Sport Taekwondo

Also known as Olympic taekwondo, this style emphasizes sparring and competitive techniques. Athletes train for tournaments and focus on point scoring and athletic performance.

Self-Defense Taekwondo

Concentrates on practical self-defense techniques derived from taekwondo. This style teaches students how to defend themselves in real-life situations using taekwondo principles.

Hapkido

While not strictly taekwondo, hapkido incorporates taekwondo techniques alongside joint locks, throws, and grappling. It focuses on self-defense and practical applications in various scenarios.

Types of Taekwondo Classes

Children's Taekwondo Classes

Children's Taekwondo Classes introduce basic movements in a fun and engaging manner, improving coordination, balance, and motor skills. These classes teach respect, discipline, and self-control, providing a foundation in martial arts for children aged 5 and up.

Teens Taekwondo Classes

Teens Taekwondo Classes offer advanced techniques and dynamic exercises tailored to teenagers. These classes build confidence and self-esteem through achievement and progress, promoting physical fitness and self-discipline.
